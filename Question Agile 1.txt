What is Sprint ?
Simple definition:
A sprint is a time-boxed iteration (usually 1–4 weeks) where a specific set of features is developed, tested, and completed.
<------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------->
Q4.What is Sprint Velocity
Sprint Velocity is the amount of work a team completes in one sprint, usually measured in story points.
Example:
Sprint 1 → Completed 20 story points
Sprint 2 → Completed 25 story points
Sprint 3 → Completed 30 story points

👉 Average Sprint Velocity = (20 + 25 + 30) / 3 = 25 story points

So, your team can handle around 25 story points per sprint.

Q8.What is Epic?
An Epic in Agile is a large piece of work that is too big to complete in a single sprint, so it is broken down into smaller user stories.
